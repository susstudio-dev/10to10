Parent questions

Everything parents ask us

What are the camp dates?+

April 1 – May 31, 2026. Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 1 PM. Weekends off. You can join weekly, monthly, or for the full 2 months.

Is food provided?+

Yes. All passes include a healthy snack break. The Monthly and Full Camp passes also include one hot meal daily. Please inform us of any allergies during enrollment.

Is there transportation?+

Pick-up and drop-off can be arranged within a 5 km radius of Khammam for an additional fee of ₹999/month. Please confirm availability when booking.

Can my child join mid-week?+

Absolutely! We prorate the weekly fee so your child can join any day. Reach out on WhatsApp and we'll get you sorted in minutes.

Is a trial session available?+

Yes — we offer a free 2-hour trial session for Monthly and Full Camp enrollments so your child can meet the team before committing.

How many kids per batch?+

We cap each age group at 20 kids with a 1:8 staff-to-child ratio, so every child gets personal attention and stays safe.

What should my child bring?+

Just a water bottle, a change of clothes, and a spare pair of socks. We provide all art supplies, games, mats, and the camp kit.

Are there any hidden fees?+

None. The listed price includes everything except optional transportation. Field trip costs are covered in the Full Camp pass.
