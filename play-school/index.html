Common questions

Before you enroll.

What is the minimum age for admission?+

We accept children from 18 months for Playgroup. They should ideally be walking confidently and able to spend ~3 hours away from a parent.

What are the school timings?+

Playgroup and Nursery: 9 AM – 12 PM. LKG and UKG: 9 AM – 12:30 PM. We're open Monday to Friday. Saturday is reserved for parent-engagement events.

Do you provide transport?+

Currently no. We're located on Mamatha College Road which is central enough that most families do their own pickup. We're happy to coordinate carpools.

What about food and snacks?+

We provide a fresh, nutritionist-approved snack mid-morning. Lunch is parent-packed. We strictly enforce a no-junk-food policy and accommodate allergies.

What happens if my child is sick?+

Please keep them home if they have fever, cough or diarrhea. We send a sick child home immediately to protect everyone. Make-up days for prolonged illness are case-by-case.

What is the vacation calendar?+

Term 1: June – Sept · Term 2: Oct – Dec · Term 3: Jan – March. Standard Indian holidays plus a 2-week summer break in April.

What is the fee refund policy?+

Full refund within 7 days of enrollment if your child has not attended a single session. After attendance begins, fees are non-refundable but transferable to a sibling.

How often are parent-teacher meetings?+

Quarterly formal PTMs plus an open-door policy any time. We also host monthly themed parent workshops on topics like screen time, nutrition, sleep.

Is there a uniform?+

Yes — comfortable, weather-appropriate, included in the annual fee. We avoid restrictive clothing that hampers free movement.

Can I observe a class before deciding?+

Absolutely. We offer a free trial day where your child joins a real class. Many parents also stay on a back-bench to observe directly.
